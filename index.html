  • What is RECOIN Project ?

    The Recoin project is a real asset tokenization project in the form of Apartment Property to be integrated into digital assets through the use of blockchain technology which is hereinafter called RECOIN.

    ReCoin then developed 2 types of tokens, namely ReCoin Asset (RCA) and ReCoin Rent (RCR), both of which have different goals but each support each other in the development of a wider apartment property ecosystem.

  • What is the difference between ReCoin Asset (RCA) and ReCoin Rent (RCR) both in terms of function and purpose of issuance ?

    ReCoin Asset (RCA) is a token issued as a representation of ownership of real assets in the form of Apartment Property whose value in the future will fluctuate greatly because it is greatly influenced by the level of public trust which is reflected in demand and supply. ReCoin Asset (RCA) ownership aims to provide access to services or products in the ReCoin blockchain ecosystem as well as investment vehicles that have profit potential but are not considered financial instruments.

    ReCoin Rent (RCR) is a token issued as a digital representation of Apartment Property according to existing availability, so RCR is categorized as an RWA token. In contrast to RCA, the value of the RCR Token does not fluctuate because from the start it has been pegged at a ratio of 1 RCR = IDR 1,000,- where the amount of supply issued refers to the availability of apartment properties that are ready to be used.

  • What is the difference between ReCoin Asset (RCR) and Recoin Rent (RCR) in terms of target users ?

    In principle, these two tokens can be widely used by all groups. However, if you look at existing trends, Recoin Asset (RCA) is more suitable for those who have investment goals, while Recoin Rent (RCR) is aimed at those who need apartment facilities to use. Although on the other hand RCR can be a business, because ownership of RCR is the same as ownership of apartment rental income.
